Vendor Payment Approval Workflow

Finance updated the vendor payment approval workflow for Q3. Invoices above 50,000 USD require two approvals: the Finance Analyst validates purchase order matching, and the Finance Director approves payment release.

The workflow adds a segregation-of-duties control. The employee who creates a vendor record cannot approve payment to that vendor in the same cycle.

Emergency vendor payments must include a risk note and a post-payment review within two business days.
