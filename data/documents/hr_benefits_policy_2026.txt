Employee Benefits Policy — Fiscal Year 2026

Classification: Internal
Department: Human Resources
Effective Date: January 1, 2026

This policy outlines the employee benefits program for all full-time employees. Part-time employees working 20 or more hours per week are eligible for a subset of benefits as noted below.

Health Insurance:
All full-time employees are enrolled in the company health plan, which includes medical, dental, and vision coverage. The employer covers 85% of the premium for individual plans and 70% for family plans. Open enrollment runs from November 1 through November 30 each year. Mid-year changes require a qualifying life event.

Retirement Savings:
The company matches employee 401(k) contributions up to 6% of base salary. Matching contributions vest over a four-year schedule: 25% per year. Employees are automatically enrolled at a 3% contribution rate unless they opt out within 30 days of hire.

Paid Time Off:
Full-time employees receive 15 days of PTO in years 1 through 3, 20 days in years 4 through 7, and 25 days after 7 years. PTO accrues monthly and may be carried over up to 5 days into the following year. Unused PTO beyond the carryover limit is forfeited on December 31.

Parental Leave:
Primary caregivers receive 16 weeks of paid parental leave. Secondary caregivers receive 6 weeks. Both must be taken within 12 months of the qualifying event.

Professional Development:
Each employee receives a $2,500 annual professional development budget for conferences, certifications, and courses. Reimbursement requires manager approval and must be submitted within 60 days of the expense.

Remote Work:
Employees in eligible roles may work remotely up to 3 days per week. Remote work arrangements must be approved by the direct manager and reviewed quarterly. Equipment stipends of $500 are available for home office setup in the first year.

Changes for 2026:
- Mental health benefit expanded: 12 therapy sessions per year covered at 100%, increased from 8.
- Fertility treatment coverage added up to $15,000 lifetime maximum.
- Student loan repayment assistance: $100 per month employer contribution, up to $10,000 lifetime.
